MathApp Privacy Policy

1. INTRODUCTION

Aratani ("we", "us") is the creator of MathApp ("the Application"), an application designed to test users' mathematical skills in a safe and fun environment.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTION

As we value the privacy of our users, we do not collect, store, share, or use any personal information while you use MathApp. The Application does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

MathApp does not integrate with or share information with third-party services.

4. ADVERTISING

MathApp does not use targeted advertising nor does it collect data for this purpose.

5. HOSTING

The Application is hosted on a server that does not collect user data.

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Please review this policy regularly for any changes.
